Hello.

I'm Kenny Bruins, a Visual, UX, and Graphic Designer based in the Inland Empire, CA. I studied Graphic Design & Studio Art at California State University San Bernardino. I'm all about the power of details, big or small, and how they can impact the user's experience.

I've been working in the promotional/custom products industry for over 7 years and have worked with clients from a broad range of industries from food & beverage, beauty, entertainment, all the way to manufacturing. This has lead me to adapt to different requirements for the diverse user/customer base that I design for.

My career has taken me through various skillsets like web design, email design, 3D design, photography, presentations, UI/UX, and visual identity. Throughout my journey, I've learned the value of design thinking and putting the user first. My skillset and background helps my end clients and their users reach their goals faster and more effectively.

When I'm not busy advocating for your users, you can catch me strumming on the guitar or hitting up shows. I also love exploring hiking trails in local national parks and taking photos of the world around me. Taking a break from the design world and immersing myself in nature helps me focus on what truly matters in my work and focus of my clients goals.

Thanks for taking the time to read my story. I hope to hear from you soon!
